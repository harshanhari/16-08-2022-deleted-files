About Our Dog Care Services

PUPcare system came into being as an idea to help people raise their pets. CEO & Founder, Harshan Hari has been an avid dog lover and a breeder all his life. While working at his developer job, he was frequently asked to advise on pet keeping. He identified the knowledge gap and thought it would be a great idea to start putting this information in the public domain and involve other pet owners to share their experiences as well. PUPcare system started as an online community that told people to care puppies, breeding and raising pets in the Indian context.
